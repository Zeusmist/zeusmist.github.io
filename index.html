About Me

Creation is magic!
As a child, I would crack open toys to see what made them special (I still do this). Be it synthetic fiber or wires I found inside, I would be fascinated and wonder why they were there and what their purpose was.
In my early teenage years, I would search google for how applications I loved were created. I would read different posts on the creation of similar applications. At first, a lot of what I read was very confusing, but over time I understood because I started taking programming courses on free platforms (The first language I learned was C++).
After high school, the love for software grew stronger because I knew how to write code. So, I went on to get an Advanced Diploma in Software Engineering. After tons of group and individual projects, the love increased. Yes, there were days I felt like programming was not for me or software development was hard, but the joy of looking at a completed project knowing how much hard work went into its creation, surpasses all forms of doubt.
I then proceeded to get a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science.
Despite the fact I have degrees on the way, the love for software engineering makes me learn more and update what I already know.
